4: Reading on Your Daintree E-Reader

Optimize your Daintree E-Reader's settings to enjoy a comfortable, intuitive reading experience.

For information about using the touch display to download and view documents, see Chapter 2: Navigating Your Daintree E-Reader.

Customize Text Display

To adjust the appearance of content to match your specific reading situation and preferences, use the Text Display menu.

Important: Changes you make to the Text Display settings are device-wide; they apply to all books in your Daintree Library.

Access the Text Display Menu

  1. Open a book or document from your Daintree Library.

    For help accessing content in the Daintree Library, see Chapter 3: Managing Content.

  2. Tap the top of the screen to display the toolbar.
  3. Tap the Text Display icon to open the Text Display menu.

Style Options in the Text Display Menu

Choose to do any of the following:

  1. Tap on the Font tab.
  2. See the instructions below to change any of the following:
    • Style: Tap the arrow to the right of Font Family. Then tap the button to select a font style.
    • Weight: Tap the arrow to the right of Bold. Then tap the sign to increase the font weight or the sign to decrease the font weight.
    • Size: Tap the arrow to the right of Size. Then tap the sign to increase the font size or the sign to decrease the font size.
  1. Tap the Layout tab.
  2. See the instructions below to change any of the following:
    • Page Orientation: Below Orientation, tap to select portrait or landscape mode.
    • Page Margins: Below Margins, tap to select narrow, regular, or wide margins.
  1. Tap the Layout tab.
  2. See the instructions below to change any of the following:
    • Text Alignment: Below Alignment, tap to select justified or left-aligned text.
    • Page Margins: Below Spacing, tap to select narrow, regular, or wide spacing.
